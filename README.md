# Retail-Analysis-KPI-Dashboard
A KPI-driven retail sales dashboard analyzing revenue, product performance, and trends using Excel, designed to provide actionable business insights through visual reporting
**Objectives**
Monitor overall sales and revenue performance

Identify top-performing products and categories

Analyze sales trends over time

Provide a clear KPI view for business stakeholders

**Key Features**
KPIs Tracked: Total Sales, Revenue, Orders, Top Products

**Visualizations:**
Sales & revenue trends

Top 10 products by performance

Category-wise contribution

Interactive Elements: Filters and slicers for easy data exploration

Clean Layout: Business-friendly dashboard design

**Tools & Technologies**
Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

Charts & Graphs

Slicers & Filters

Basic data cleaning and transformation

**Dataset**
Retail sales dataset containing product, category, quantity, price, and transaction-level details

Dataset is included in the repository for reference and reproducibility

**Insights Derived**
Identification of high-revenue products and categories

Understanding sales distribution and trends

Quick visibility into business performance through KPIs

**Learning Outcomes**
Practical experience in KPI identification and dashboard design

Hands-on Excel-based data analysis

Improved understanding of retail business metrics
